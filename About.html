Restaurant

From the pan...Into the fire.

At Pots & Pan, we know that life is better together and everyone is happiest when they’re with family. From never ending servings of our freshly baked breadsticks and iconic garden salad, to our homemade soups and sauces, there’s something for everyone to enjoy.

Over 3 Decades,Pots & Pan has pioneered Italian cuisine in India. With 50 outlets in 4 countries, we are India’s largest home grown italian brand bringing you a delicious menu of Pizzas, Pastas, Appetizers & much more.Italian food is simple yet sophisticated, using few ingredients to make a dish so flavourful you keep coming back for more. Inspired by Italy’s finest family chefs, we bring you the warmth & love of Italian Cuisine directly to your home.

OUR PROMISE

We Value Quality over Quantity

Italians love their food, they have a deep understanding of it and how it should be prepared & eaten. Inspired by traditional Italian cooking & processes we at Pots & Pan offer a menu backed by the finest Italian chefs & quality ingredients.We have our own cheese factory and source our ingredients from suppliers that offer the freshest produce, making it possible for us to serve you, the Italian way
